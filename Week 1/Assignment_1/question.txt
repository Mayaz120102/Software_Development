Difference between List and Dictionary of Pyhton are given below:

1. A list is an ordered collection of items that are used to store multiple items in a single variable . A Dictionary is a collection which is ordered ,stores data in key-value pairs 
2. List access by index . Dictionary access by key
3. Duplicates are allowed . Duplicates are not allowed keys unique
4. List = [1,2,3,4,4,5] . Dictionary ={"Name": "Abrar", "Proffession" :"Studnet"}
